# Node Neo Privacy Policy

**The canonical, user-facing Privacy Policy for Node Neo lives at:**

## → <https://nodeneo.ai/privacy.html>

That URL is the version App Store reviewers see, the version the in-app *Settings → Legal & Resources → Privacy Policy* link opens, and the version every store-listing privacy declaration cross-references. Treating the website as the single source of truth keeps the user-facing privacy commitment from drifting between two surfaces.

---

## In one line, for the impatient

Node Neo runs entirely on your device. **ABSGrafx LLC** — the publisher of Node Neo — operates no servers, collects no analytics, and has no account system. Your wallet keys, chat history, and preferences never leave your device unless you explicitly send a chat message to a third-party AI provider (and even then, the message goes directly to the provider you chose, not to us).

For the long-form treatment of what stays on device, what third parties Node Neo necessarily talks to (Base RPC, Morpheus model registry, your selected AI provider, Apple's App Store flow), iOS permission strings, App Store nutrition-label mapping, and your rights — see the canonical website version above.
