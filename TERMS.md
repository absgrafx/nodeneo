# Node Neo Terms of Use

**The canonical, user-facing Terms of Use for Node Neo live at:**

## → <https://nodeneo.ai/terms.html>

That URL is the version App Store reviewers see, the version the in-app *Settings → Legal & Resources → Terms of Service* link opens, and the version any change-notice in `RELEASE_NOTES.md` will reference.
